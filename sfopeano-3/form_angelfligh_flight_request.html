What you may bring:

Small aircraft such as the kind typically flown on an Angel Flight have different weather restrictions than commercial aircraft.

Pilots make the final decisions about the execution of their missions. A pilot may delay or cancel a mission because of bad weather or other critical factors.

You must have a back-up plan or to be able to re-schedule your appointments.

Purpose of flight:

Here are some examples of medical flights we provide:
  • To and from advanced treatment for disease not available locally
  • Traveling to and from pre or post transplant appointments
  • Transplant (with a large time window, pending availability)
  • Clinical trials
  • Surgeries
  • Expert consultations
  • Other
Here are some examples of the compassion flights we provide:
  • Domestic Violence Relocation
  • Special Needs Camps
  • Wounded Warriors
  • Disaster relief
  • Caretaker for someone very ill
  • Pre adoption visits
  • Traveling to see someone in end stages
What we can't accommodate:
  • Funerals
  • Relocations
  • Air ambulance needs
  • Short notice, short window transplant flights
